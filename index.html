Services

How do we operate

1

Web

Dedicated manager works with you directly. We sign Non-Disclosure Agreement. Identify your project requirements and go over the user stories. We provide the estimate and sign the development contract. Dedicated IT team is assigned to your project.

2

Design

We prepare a scheme and design User Interface for your web project or application. Once you confirm the design, we move over to the development phase.

3

Development & Testing

Quality Assurance team reviews the functionality and tracks possible bugs. Project manager informs you about the progress and you evaluate the results.

4

Support

The website or application is ready to be published on a live domain or server. Mobile application is ready to be deployed live on Google Play or Apple. Congratulations – we are going live now.
